CMSD LEGEND OF THE MONTH

Ron Addison

I have always been proud that I was born and raised in Cleveland. When people asked me where I am from I always say Cleveland, the city not the suburbs. When I was growing up the big names in Cleveland Sports were Jim Brown and Rocky Colavito. I lived 6 miles from Cleveland Stadium where I would watch the Indiansplay with 2,000 people in the stands.My friends and I spent all fall playing football, all winter playing basketball outside and all spring and summer playing baseball. I was naturally drawn to running. I just always loved it. My mom would give me a quarter and tell me to run down to the corner.
